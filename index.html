Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy for Planeventz ("we," "us," or "our") explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Planeventz is an event management and memory-sharing platform that allows users to create events, invite participants, and securely upload and view photos and videos.

If you have any questions, contact us at tech.planeventz@gmail.com.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect the following information:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (for authentication/OTP)
  • Profile details
  • Event-related data

Device & Usage Data

  • Device type and OS version
  • IP address
  • Usage logs
  • Crash reports
  • Notification tokens

Permissions We Request

Camera Access:

  • Capture photos in the app
  • Upload event memories

Media / Storage Access (Read):

  • Select and upload existing media files

Contacts Access (Read):

  • Invite guests to events
  • Suggest contacts
  • We do NOT store your full contact list on our servers

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage accounts
  • Enable event participation
  • Upload and display media
  • Send notifications
  • Improve app performance
  • Prevent fraud and misuse

3. End-to-End Encryption (E2EE)

Planeventz uses end-to-end encryption for photos and videos:

  • Media is encrypted on your device before upload
  • Encryption keys are not accessible to Planeventz
  • Data is encrypted during transmission and storage
  • Only authorized users can access the content

This ensures that even Planeventz cannot view your private media.

4. Third-Party Services

Firebase Analytics:

  • Used to understand app usage
  • Helps improve performance and features

Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM):

  • Sends notifications, invitations, and updates

5. Data Sharing & Selling

We respect your privacy:

  • We DO NOT sell your personal data to any third-party platform.
  • We do not rent or trade user data for marketing purposes.

We only share data when necessary:

  • With service providers (e.g., Firebase)
  • For legal compliance
  • During business transfers

6. Data Retention

We retain data as long as necessary to provide services or comply with legal obligations.

7. Data Security

  • Secure authentication
